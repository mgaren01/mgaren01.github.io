Week One

The struggle, as they say, is real. And as the introductory materials promised, I did indeed encounter deep feelings of discomfort. A few days ago, I might have come off as a deal more cocky about how this whole process was going to go for me. I don't feel that way now, that's for sure. But that's a good thing. It means I've learned the reality of what it's going to take to get through. And it also means I've learned enough that I can reasonably expect someone to see this message on teh inturnetz. Put in briefer terms, my biggest struggle this week has been against my own foibles. Oh, and the abstractness of much of the material. Can't forget that. So no, the experience hasn't been quite what I expected, but it didn't break me either. And now that I'm here, I'm very much looking forward to exploring the logical side of things with pseudo-coding, which I think may help things flow a little smoother in future.
