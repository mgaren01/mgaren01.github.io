Week Eight


They're all rough weeks at this point. Though I know this was intended more as an 'intake' week, as opposed to one of output, it's still hard not to feel as though I haven't accomplished much. Maybe I've learned more than I think; I don't know. I suppose the next couple of weeks will tell.
I guess I'm just not used to thinking about things at the level of abstraction we're talking about; every new thing seems increasingly difficult to 'see' in my head. It's not so easy to put something like 'class' in a box, and even less so to see ways to make use of such a nebulous idea.
I suppose, for the moment, the only thing to do is keep trying, and hope things sink in with more practice.
